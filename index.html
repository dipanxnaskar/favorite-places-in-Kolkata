My Favorite Places in Kolkata

Explore the charm and beauty of the City of Joy!

Victoria Memorial

An iconic white marble monument built in memory of Queen Victoria. Surrounded by beautiful gardens, it reflects the colonial past of Kolkata.

Indian Museum

The oldest and largest museum in India, housing rare collections of antiques, fossils, and art that showcase India’s cultural heritage.

Science City

A popular attraction for all ages featuring interactive exhibits, 3D shows, and thrilling science demonstrations.

Eco Park

A vast urban park with boating, cycling, gardens, and replicas of world wonders — perfect for relaxation and fun.

St. Paul’s Cathedral

A magnificent Anglican cathedral known for its Gothic architecture and peaceful ambiance.

Zoological Garden, Alipore

The oldest zoo in India, home to a wide variety of animals and birds — a favorite spot for families and nature lovers.
